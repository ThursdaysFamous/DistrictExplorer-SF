# Redistricting Runbook — pointer

The **master lives in the Chicago repo** (owner: CHI, applies to every
metro), at
[`DistrictExplorer-CHI/docs/REDISTRICTING_RUNBOOK.md`](https://github.com/ThursdaysFamous/DistrictExplorer-CHI/blob/main/docs/REDISTRICTING_RUNBOOK.md).

This fork deliberately carries no copy (full copies drift; the response
procedure is metro-agnostic by design). The per-fork half of the system —
**when to look** — is this repo's own [`WATCH.md`](../WATCH.md): SF's
redistricting calendar, the SFUSD attendance-area drill, and the
per-election voting-sites refresh. Work the runbook's numbered steps from
the master whenever a WATCH trigger fires.
